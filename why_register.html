Why should you register?

Submitting your mHealth solution into the mRegistry offers a number of benefits:

  • Gain a registry ID that can be used by you, the mHealth community, and other organizations as a way to uniquely refer to your mHealth strategy or project.
  • Gain a visual representation of your mHealth project or strategy that is helpful as a way to be able to articulate your approach to different stakeholders and across sectors
  • Ensure that partner institutions which house additional information on specific deployments, institutions funding mHealth, and health journals are able to refer to the unique identifier.
  • Ensure that WHO mTERG is able to catalogue the mHealth strategies utilized, and appropriately link project or strategy based evidence of value.
